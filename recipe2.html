The bomb dot net

I'm going to tell you a secret. I hate lasagna. I never felt like the hour investment of preparing all the stuff paid off for the same taste you could get with speghetti. Not only that but it had to bake for another hour! Like, why?

But then there's this recipe! It really makes everything worth it. Just writing this makes me want to have my wife make it right now, just kidding, I'm the chef here.

What makes this lasagna worthy of the last meal is the "cheese" sauce. The basil, lemon, nutritional yeast, and dijon mustard come together to form this delicious concoction. I sometimes make a double serving just so I can dip the boiled lasagna noodles in it while the lasagna bakes. But know while you're snacking you're eating whole foods, not actual fattening cheese.

Make sure you season the veggies with salt and pepper. Nothing is worse than plain vegetables. Gross. Plenty or salt, plenty of pepper ensure when they go in the lasagna you'll taste them. Also dont overcook the veggies. You want them soft but not limply overdone.

Once you've put everything together, you'll have an overwhelming sense of excitement wash over, dont worry, its normal, you're about to enjoy a five star meal.
